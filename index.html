Why you should Learn Music Theory

Music theory is something you must know if you want a good and solid start to your music life. Whether you are starting a new instrument, making your own music or just want to learn something new (as learning music theory has big creative benefit), this website is where you can find your starting point. It is a one-stop resource for musicians and music-lovers alike. Our goal is to provide a comprehensive, easy-to-navigate platform for all your music-related needs. Whether you’re looking to learn more about the history of music or brush up on your theory skills, you’ll find it all here.
